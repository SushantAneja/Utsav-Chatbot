How Our Chatbot Works?

  • Events are suggested and filtered as a pereson searches them in the search bar.
  • Once event is submitted, details like slots, timings and more are displayed.
  • The chatbot does this by searching for the entered event in the database provided by the developers.
  • With the help of algorithms, our chatbot is able to convert the data into suitable format and display it.
